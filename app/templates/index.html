Welcome to the Ultimate Battle of Ship Commanders!

Welcome to the greatest Battleship game of all time!
Rules are simple:

Whoever sinks all the ships that belong to his opponent wins!

    Markings on the board:
  • '*' - unshot part of the ocean
  • 'X' - hit
  • 'O' - miss

    There are 7 ships to sink:
  • one Aircraft Carries which takes 5 parts of the ocean
  • one Batleship which takes 4 parts of the ocean
  • one Submarine which takes 3 parts of the ocean
  • two Destroyers which take 2 parts of the ocean each
  • two Patrol Boats which take 1 part of the ocean each

The ships cannot touch each other in any way (horizontally, vertically or across)
